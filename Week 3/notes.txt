Basic workflow: 

1. Have a design brief ready. 
2. Research/competitor research, have ideal target market pin pointed. 
3. Initial sketching
4. Wire framing
5. A/B testing (find software/programme where testing can be done online)
6. Feedback, revisit if needed 

7. Gather data 
8. Write down psudocode
9. Basic HTML/CSS/JS . Make it responsive
10. Testing, check console often. Debug 
11. Present idea/concept to client or tester. Respond to feedback
12. Final debug, upload project
